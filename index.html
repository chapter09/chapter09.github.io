 About Me
I am a second-year Ph.D candidate of the ECE Department in University of Toronto. My supervisor is Prof. Baochun Li.

I received my BE/ME degrees both from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2012 and 2015, respectively. From 2013 to 2014, I was a visiting student at System and networkING (SING) Lab in HKUST working with Prof. Kai Chen.
